About me

I am a researcher specialising in computational linguistics and language acquisition. My work focuses on the intersection of cognitive science and natural language processing.

I have published extensively in peer-reviewed journals and presented at international conferences across Europe and North America.
  • 2018–presentAssociate Professor, University of BarcelonaDepartment of Linguistics
  • 2014–2018Postdoctoral Researcher, MITBrain and Cognitive Sciences
  • 2010–2014PhD in Linguistics, Oxford UniversityThesis: "Contextual Disambiguation in NLP"
  • 2007–2010BA & MA, University of BarcelonaMajor: Catalan and Spanish Philology

Publications

Contextual Ambiguity in Multilingual Neural Models (2024)
Journal of Computational Linguistics · Vol. 50, pp. 112–138
This paper investigates how large language models handle contextual ambiguity across multilingual settings, with a focus on Romance languages.
Prosodic Cues in Second Language Acquisition (2022)
Language Learning · Vol. 72, pp. 45–89
An empirical study of how adult learners of Catalan use prosodic information during speech perception.
Discourse Coherence and Reading Comprehension (2020)
Applied Psycholinguistics · Vol. 41, pp. 789–812
This study examines the role of discourse coherence markers in reading comprehension across three age groups.
