About me

I am a predoctoral researcher at Universitat Pompeu Fabra working on territorial identities, nationalism, and secessionism. My research combines qualitative and quantitative methods to examine how education, literacy, and generational change shape political identities in stateless nations.

I have presented my work at international conferences and published in journals such as Territory, Politics, and Governance and South European Politics and Society. I have also been a visiting researcher at Georgetown University.
